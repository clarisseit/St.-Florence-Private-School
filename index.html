About Us

St. Florence Private School

Brief Excerpts of the School

St. Florence Private School is a beacon of hope for families in the lower-income bracket, offering quality education at an affordable cost. Led by the vibrant and dedicated headmistress, Mrs. Olapade.  The school is committed to ensuring that every child has access to a transformative learning experience. Through personalized attention and a nurturing environment, we strive to unlock the full potential of each child

Our Mission

At St. Florence Private school, our mission is to empower every student, regardless of background, religion or culture, with the knowledge and skills to succeed academically and beyond. We envision a future where all children have access to high quality education, laying the foundation for a brighter tomorrow
